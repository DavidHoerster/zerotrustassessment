Step 1: (Optional) Zero Trust Assessment

Begin with the Zero Trust Assessment to ensure your organization is securely positioned before advancing your Zero Trust journey.
This assessment provides essential checks to confirm a strong security baseline, preparing you for advanced features and a more resilient security posture.
Click the video on the left to learn more about the assessment or click the button below for in-depth documentation.

Step 2: Prepare for the Workshop

  1. Review the our Introduction to the Zero Trust Workshop video on the right
  2. Identify which teams in your organization to involve.
  3. Access the Zero Trust architecture resources here.
  4. Reach out to your Microsoft partner or account team, if applicable, about including them in your workshop.
  5. Download the Zero Trust Workshop tool by clicking the button below.
